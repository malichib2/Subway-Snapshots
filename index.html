About Me

"As a lifelong enthusiast of all things locomotive, I have always had a special affinity for the iconic trains of New York City. There's something about the rumble of the subway beneath my feet, the whistle of the Long Island Rail Road as it speeds through the city, or the majestic Hudson River views from the windows of the Metro-North Express that gets my heart racing. Whether I'm commuting to work in Manhattan, exploring the outer boroughs, or simply enjoying a leisurely ride to Coney Island, I find solace and excitement in the rhythms and routines of New York's train systems. With their storied history, colorful characters, and ever-changing landscapes, New York City's trains have become an integral part of my daily life - and I feel grateful to call this bustling metropolis my home."
